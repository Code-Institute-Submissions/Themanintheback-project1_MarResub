About Us

welcome

Hello and welcome to How To Pasta. We are a small but mighty group of enthusiastic lovers of pasta and would like spread that joy to the people of London by teaching you all how to make fresh pasta.


We provide an exclusive venue for our cooking classes which comes with a fully equipped kitchen so there will be no need for you to bring anything with you. Prepare to become a professioanl in being able to create your own pasta.

In the class

We will take you through a step by step process of how to make your own pasta dough using fresh eggs and plain flour. You will also be introduced to the tools used to make your own pasta.


We will be inviting you to create your shapes of pasta as well as the standard shapes of pasta. In the second half of the class as well as making the basic shapes of pasta we will be introducing you to how to make Gnocchi, Garganelli, Pappardelle and Ravioli.

Why learn to make pasta

It's Healthier

Making pasta with fresh ingredients provides better nutritional value and easier on your body's digestion system.

Quick to make

Making pasta does not taking that much time or effort to make. It can be made without a pasta machine and done entirely by hand and can be made into any shape you want.

It Tastes better

Fresh pasta taste far better than store bought pasta. Because of the use of eggs in the dough it provides the pasta with afresh taste.

It costs less

It costs more for you to buy pasta than it is to make it. The only ingredents you need are flour, eggs and salt. All things you already have in your home.
